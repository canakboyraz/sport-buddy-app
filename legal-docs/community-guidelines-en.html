Community Guidelines

Last Updated: December 4, 2024

At Sport Buddy, we are committed to building a safe, inclusive, and respectful community for sports enthusiasts. These guidelines outline the behavior we expect from all our users.

1. Respect and Inclusivity

  • Be Respectful: Treat all users with respect and dignity. Harassment, hate speech, bullying, or discrimination of any kind will not be tolerated.
  • Be Inclusive: Sport Buddy is for everyone, regardless of skill level, background, or identity. Welcome new players and help them feel comfortable.
  • No Harassment: Do not engage in unwanted contact, stalking, or aggressive behavior towards other users.

2. Safety First

  • Personal Safety: When meeting for sports sessions, prioritize your safety. Meet in public places and let someone know where you are going.
  • No Violence: Threats of violence or actual violence are strictly prohibited and will result in an immediate ban.
  • Illegal Activities: Do not use Sport Buddy for any illegal purposes or to promote illegal acts.

3. Content Guidelines

  • Appropriate Content: Do not post content that is sexually explicit, violent, or offensive. This includes profile photos, session descriptions, and chat messages.
  • No Spam: Do not use the platform for unauthorized advertising, spamming, or solicitation.
  • Truthfulness: Provide accurate information in your profile and session details. Do not impersonate others.

4. Fair Play

  • Show Up: If you join a session, make every effort to attend. If you cannot make it, leave the session in the app as soon as possible to allow others to join.
  • Honest Ratings: Rate other users fairly and honestly based on their behavior and sportsmanship.

5. Enforcement

We take these guidelines seriously. Violations may result in:

  • Content removal
  • Temporary account suspension
  • Permanent account ban
  • Reporting to law enforcement if necessary

6. Reporting

If you see something that violates these guidelines, please report it using the in-app reporting tools or contact us at support@sportbuddy.app.
